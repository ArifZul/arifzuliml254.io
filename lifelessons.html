First of all, our life is very challenging. It is full of barrier and sometimes has lot of problems and conflicts. It is necessary to think wisely before making a final decision for your life in future. As a preparation, we need to think of a lot of aspects before decided to do something, especially if it is serious consideration. For example, education is a serious thing to consider because it may picture your bright future.


Picture 2: Depression may give negative impact for your mental health


Furthermore, in our life, it will involve communication no matter what you do, either mouth-to-mouth communication or physical communication. So, we should train our communication skills because it is really important in many aspects, including education, industry, or having a small talk.

In other point of view, we should appreciate our time with families and friends because they are your motivation for making you stronger. We should have positive vibes between each other, so it will heal your emotion. They may also give you some spirit to keep moving forward because they can give motivation when you at the downside or difficulties on your life.

Besides that, we should be more focus in studying and education no matter wherever or whenever if necessary. The education is one of the crucial aspects in making up our self-discipline, also as preparation for future consideration. Studying is essential in education and it must be balance to achieve success in the subject or field that we learn.


Summary

There are so many aspects we need to think in our life, which are:-
  • Social - Avoid social problems
  • Communication - Essentials in daily routine
  • Education and discipline - Develop discipline and focus in education
  • Close contacts - Keep engaging with them to gain trust and encouragement
